Hey there! I’m Gianna, and music has always been the place where I feel most alive. If there’s one thing that defines me, then it’s music. I’ve played as a keyboardist in a band, performed in front of crowds, and even had moments where it was just me, a keyboard, and a story to tell through sound. Whether I’m on stage or practicing alone, music is my comfort, my passion, and the part of myself I love sharing with others. For me, music isn’t just a hobby to me because it is where I express who I am without saying a word.
